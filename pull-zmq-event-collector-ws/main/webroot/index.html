Coloring My SoundTouch Events

What is this visualization ? It's a representation of the live WebSocket notifications that are sent from my Bose SoundTouch at home.

Hover over the dots in the The SoundTouch Events visualization to see what kinds of events these dots correspond to. The Rolling Events log keeps track of the last 10 events.
